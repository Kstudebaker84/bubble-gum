About

Thanks for visiting our site! Our products are designed for grown-ups who are still kids at heart and will dare to take a tasty adventure!

Located in northeast Ohio, we have been in business since 1984. Founder and CEO Kristen Studebaker had a vision to reinvent chewing gum for the 21st century. She dreamed that bubble gum would no longer be minty or fruity. She wanted more out of her bubble gum. Having sold millions of packs of bubble gum since the company's inception, Sizzling Bubble Gum has become an American staple.

Find Sizzling Bubble Gum in your local retailer wherever chewing gum is sold.
