Bloody Mary

A Bloody Mary contains vodka, tomato juice, and a combination of spices including Worcestershire sauce, Hot sauce, garlic, and lemon; this cocktail pairs nicely with the Applewood and black pepper flavors that only Sizzling Bacon Bubble Gum can offer!

Sauvignon Blanc

The red wine, Sauvignon Blanc, has fruit flavors of green apple, passion fruit, and flowery peach. This proves to be an excellent after-gum experience to balance the palate after experiencing our mouthwatering Russian Beef Stroganoff Gum.

Corona

This Mexican pale lager will pair nicely with its smooth taste before, during, or after chewing our Sizzling Spicy Mexican Chicken Gum. Squeeze an extra lime into your Corona if you chew while drinking - the lime will compliment the red pepper and chili flavors very well!

Coca-Cola

This bubbling sweet soft drink pairs perfectly with the mouth-watering taste of Sizzling BBQ Ribs Gum! The slight kick in the gum will sizzle with the carbonation and caffeine of your Coca-Cola!
